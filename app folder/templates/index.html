ASL Image Translator

American Sign Language (ASL) is the primary sign language of deaf communities in the United States, with estimated users ranging between 250,000 - 500,000 persons.

Within ASL, there is a collection of 26 signs that represent the phonetic alphabet, which is used for both proper nouns and technical terms for which no native ASL exists.

Given the static nature of the ASL alphabet, we saw an opportunity to apply machine learning techniques to train a model to interpret user-submitted images as their associated phonetic letters.
